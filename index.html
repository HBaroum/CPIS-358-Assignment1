Project Idea

StudySync is a student-focused platform that helps university students find study partners based on the same subject, study goal, and available time.

Benefits

  • Connect with classmates easily
  • Study online or face-to-face
  • Save time searching for partners
  • Improve motivation and performance

How It Works

  1. Create an account
  2. Select your course and study goal
  3. Choose your available time
  4. Find a matching study partner
