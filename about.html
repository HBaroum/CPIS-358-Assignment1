Who We Are

We are a group of students who created StudySync to help university students find suitable study partners based on their course, study goal, and free time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make studying easier, more organized, and more collaborative for university students by offering a simple and useful platform.

Project Image

This image represents students studying together.

Why We Created This Project

Many students find it difficult to coordinate with classmates for study sessions. Some students need help with assignments, while others want to prepare for quizzes or exams. StudySync helps solve this problem by making the matching process easier.

Main Features

  • Easy student matching
  • Simple login and signup pages
  • Course and goal-based connection
  • Flexible online or in-person study options
